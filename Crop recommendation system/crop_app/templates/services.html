Our Services

At Raj Agrotech, we offer a range of services to support your agricultural needs:

  • Soil Testing: Comprehensive soil analysis to determine nutrient levels and deficiencies.
  • Crop Recommendation: Personalized crop suggestions based on soil and environmental factors.
  • Farming Consultation: Expert advice on farming practices to enhance yield and sustainability.
  • Weather Forecasting: Accurate weather predictions to help plan your agricultural activities.
  • Resource Management: Efficient management of water, fertilizers, and other resources.

Contact us to learn more about how we can assist you in achieving better farming results.
